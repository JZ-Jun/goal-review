Testing-testing-testing----This project is a living repository of papers relevant to Goals for Behavior Change in HCI, a research area that examines the impact of goal-related technologies on actual behavior changes and the approaches used to support behavioral changes.

The paper has been accepted to CHI 2025. More information will be shared once the paper is published.

This repository is built based on Dr. Daniel Epstein's living repository for personal information. Thank you for your inspiration and guidance!
